Enhanced Pest Acoustic
Detection Device for Rice

ePADDY is a state-of-the-art tool designed to aid farmers and end-users in detecting insect pests in stored rice through sophisticated sound analysis. By leveraging advanced acoustic detection technology, ePADDY offers a sustainable and efficient solution for monitoring and managing insect pests, ensuring better quality and protection of stored products.

My Thesis

This prototype web application is part of the PhD thesis entitled "Enhanced monitoring of insect pests in stored rice based on their sound signature and evaluation of attractants for storage insects", submitted to the faculty of Organic Agricultural Sciences at the University of Kassel, Germany in fulfillment of the requirements for the degree of Doctor of Agricultural Sciences (Dr. agr.).
