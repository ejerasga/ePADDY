Enhanced Pest Acoustic Detection Device for Rice

ePADDY is a cutting-edge tool designed to assist farmers and end-users in detecting insect pests in stored rice through advanced sound analysis. Utilizing enhanced acoustic detection technology, ePADDY provides a sustainable and efficient solution for monitoring and managing insect pests, ensuring protection and better quality of stored grains.

System Features

Advanced Acoustic Sensors

Utilizes state-of-the-art acoustic sensors to detect even the slightest pest activities.

Real-Time Prediction

Provides instant insect prediction to help you.

Eco-Friendly

Designed with sustainability in mind, minimizing environmental impact while maximizing efficiency.

User-Friendly Interface

Easily navigate through our intuitive interface, suitable for users of all technical levels.

About ePADDY

This prototype web application is part of the PhD thesis entitled "Enhanced monitoring of insect pests in stored rice based on their sound signature and evaluation of attractants for storage insects", submitted to the faculty of Organic Agricultural Sciences at the University of Kassel, Germany in fulfillment of the requirements for the degree of Doctor of Agricultural Sciences (Dr. agr.).
